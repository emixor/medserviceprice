06 / 07 · Killer Features
Killer Features · "The Wow Factor" / Киллер-фичи

Three features no competitor in KZ offers.

Feature 01

Smart Basket
Умная корзина

multi-test cart optimizer

Drop 5–10 prescribed tests into one basket. The engine solves a traveling-purchaser problem across clinics and returns the cheapest split — factoring travel distance, not just sticker price.

  • Greedy + local-search optimizer
  • Cross-clinic bundling
  • Total trip cost in ₸
Feature 02

AI Prescription OCR
OCR рецепта

image → cart in one tap

Photograph a handwritten prescription. A vision-language model reads Cyrillic/Latin drug and test names, maps each line to the service directory and pre-fills the Smart Basket automatically.

  • VLM handwriting extraction
  • KZ / RU / EN recognition
  • One-tap cart mapping
Feature 03

Price Inflation Tracker
Трекер инфляции

14,748 history points

Every price change is versioned. Patients see 30/90/365-day trends per service, get drop alerts by email, and watch a city heatmap of where medicine is getting cheaper — or more expensive.

  • Per-service trend charts

07 / 07 · Differentiators & Roadmap
Strategic Differentiators & Roadmap / Отличия и Дорожная карта

Built for Kazakhstan, not copy-pasted from abroad.

★Local-first differentiators

"True Cost" travel index / Индекс реальной стоимости
2GIS walking/driving distance is folded into the basket optimizer. A clinic 300 ₸ cheaper but 12 km away loses — the engine shows the real total cost of the trip.
ОСМС public-quota indicator / Индикатор ОСМС
Each service is tagged with whether it is covered by the obligatory social-health-insurance quota, so patients know what is free at the point of care versus out-of-pocket.
Price-Lock Vouchers / Ваучеры фиксации цены
Inspired by MDsave — clinics can issue a voucher that freezes today's price for 7 days. Patients book with certainty; clinics fill idle capacity. A win-win market mechanic.
Trilingual from day one / Три языка с первого дня
KZ · RU · EN names and synonyms in the directory itself — not a UI translation layer bolted on later.
